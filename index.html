As a 20 year old company our objective at Ignite is to create beautiful, memorable artwork that will withhold the test of time and be something our clients love for life. We have a safe, clean environment and strive to deliver an enjoyable experience for everyone who comes and visits us.


There aren’t many rules here, but we do have two:

1. We do not tattoo anyone under the age of 18. No exceptions.
2. We do not tattoo anything that is derogatory or hateful, meaning sexist, racist, transphobic or homophobic.


We believe in the power of wandering – of living and experiencing the world. In order to never forget who we are and where we came from, we have chosen to catalogue our experiences through tattoos and hope to help others who want to do the same. As we’ve grown and changed, adventuring across the world and searching for our purposes, it was only natural we would eventually set down roots.
